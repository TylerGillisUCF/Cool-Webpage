Hello, I'm Tyler! 👋

Welcome to my digital space! I'm a Ph.D. student in Texts and Technology at the University of Central Florida, where I serve as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Center for Humanities and Digital Research (CHDR).


Coming from a background in History, I'm fascinated by the intersection of technology and the humanities. My research explores how digital tools and platforms are transforming the way we produce, consume, and understand history in our modern society.


When I'm not diving deep into digital archives or coding, you'll find me gaming, catching the latest movies, or planning my next travel adventure. I believe in bringing the same curiosity and enthusiasm I have for my hobbies into my academic work.
